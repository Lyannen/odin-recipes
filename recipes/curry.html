Kozhi Varutha Curry

Description

This recipe resembles Akira Hayama's Kozhi Varutha Curry from the anime Food Wars. It is comparable to an Indian Chicken Tikka Masala. We will cook chicken in a coconut-based sauce with several fried off spices.

Ingredients

  • Deboned chicken thighs
  • An onion
  • Fresh garlic
  • Fresh ginger
  • Spices: Turmeric, Coriander seeds, Garam Masala, Cumin, Cayenne pepper, Sea salt
  • Canned tomatoes
  • Coconut milk
  • Basmati rice
  • Fresh coriander

Steps

  1. Sear the chicken on high heat until golden-brown. Don't worry about it not being cooked all the way through yet, we will continue steaming it in the sauce later.
  2. Remove the chicken from the pan and in its stead add garlic, onions and grated ginger.
  3. Add generous amounts of Cumin and Garam Masala to the pan. Add Coriander seeds to your liking. Give the spices a moment to really touch the pan.
  4. Add the canned tomatoes as well as the remaining spices.
  5. Add the coconut milk and put the chicken back in. Let everything cook for at least 15 minutes.
  6. Serve with rice and garnish with Fresh coriander.
